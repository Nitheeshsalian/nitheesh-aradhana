Chapter One

How We Found Each Other

🐚 🌊 🐚

Two souls, two worlds — one chasing lines of code across glowing screens, the other illuminating minds in the realm of Medical Laboratory Technology. Yet the universe, in its infinite wisdom, conspired to bring them to the same table.

It was an ordinary lunch at a restaurant — the kind of afternoon that seems unremarkable until it becomes the one you tell your grandchildren about. Nitheesh looked across the room, took a quiet breath, walked over, and simply said "Hello." That one word changed everything.

Between logic gates and laboratory slides, they discovered something neither algorithm nor microscope could explain: love. And now, with the ocean breeze in their hair, they're ready to write their greatest chapter together.
